Writing a business plan is an important part of starting a business. Many people neglect this point and as a result face difficulties, mistakes and loss of budget and time. A business plan gives you stability, helps you anticipate market risks and gives you a better understanding of your own business.

  • In Business Planning, we discuss how to create a detailed business plan - your ultimate roadmap to success.
  • In Building Your Sphere of Influence, we will explore different strategies for growing your business, with an emphasis on building relationships, not leads. We will also look at how to build a Sphere of Influence business plan that works in tandem with the broader business plan you will create in the first module.
  • In Assisting Buyers and Sellers, we will look at best practice in customer service to help you work more efficiently and effectively to achieve the goals you have set out in your business plan.
  • We also look at successfully closing transactions and the intersection of property and technology.
